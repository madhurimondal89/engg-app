About Torque

Torque is a measure of the rotational force applied to an object. It's calculated as the product of force and the perpendicular distance from the axis of rotation.

τ = F × r
Where τ is torque, F is force, and r is the lever arm distance

Common Applications

  • Automotive: Engine torque specifications, wheel lug nuts
  • Manufacturing: Bolt tightening specifications
  • Aerospace: Critical fastener torque requirements
  • Machinery: Motor and gear specifications
